Lab mission

Bench-to-bedside research with clinical relevance.

The lab focuses on translating molecular findings into actionable strategies for patient care, particularly in cardiovascular disease, diabetes, cancer, infection, and biomarker discovery.

Four connected directions.

These pillars organize the lab's scientific identity, from clinical cohorts and patient questions to cellular models, omics technologies, and shared collaborations.

01

Clinical research

Cardiovascular diseases, cancer, infection, biomarkers, and clinical databases.

02

Basic research

In vitro models, molecular analyses, animal studies, PCR, DNA, RNA, and proteins.

03

Collaboration

Shared research networks, training, international partnerships, and knowledge exchange.

04

Technology

Transcriptomics, proteomics, epigenomics, pharmacogenomics, and bioinformatics.
