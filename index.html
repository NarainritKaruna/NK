I am a researcher specialising in cardiovascular pharmacology, proteomics, transcriptomics, and translational science. My work bridges the gap between discovery and clinical application, integrating mechanistic insights with biomarker identification to advance patient care. I am particularly focused on translating molecular findings into clinically actionable strategies. Through this approach, I aim to contribute to the development of precision medicine frameworks that improve patient outcomes and enable more targeted, effective therapies.
